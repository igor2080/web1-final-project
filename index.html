    Some features:
  • Form adds to a guestbook on the page
  • Can toggle the form to submit to https://www.w3schools.com/action_page.php instead
  • Form field validation
  • This list disappears on smaller devices
  • Guestbook posts get images generated based on email address
  • Posts are stored in LocalStorage
  • Posts are deletable
  • The background color is changeable from an external source
  • The Guestbook title is draggable(and so is the word in this list)
  • Auto generate N amount of posts
